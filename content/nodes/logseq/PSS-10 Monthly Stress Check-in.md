- # PSS-10 — Perceived Stress Scale (10-item)
- *Recommended frequency: monthly.*
- ---
- **The questions in this scale ask about your feelings and thoughts during the last month.**
- *Scale: 0 = Never | 1 = Almost never | 2 = Sometimes | 3 = Fairly often | 4 = Very often*
- *For each question, indicate how often you felt or thought a certain way. Items marked (R) are reverse-scored — see the scoring note below.*
- ---
- **1.** In the last month, how often have you been upset because of something that happened unexpectedly?
	- score-1:: 
- **2.** In the last month, how often have you felt that you were unable to control the important things in your life?
	- score-2:: 
- **3.** In the last month, how often have you felt nervous and stressed?
	- score-3:: 
- **4.** In the last month, how often have you felt confident about your ability to handle your personal problems? *(R)*
	- score-4:: 
- **5.** In the last month, how often have you felt that things were going your way? *(R)*
	- score-5:: 
- **6.** In the last month, how often have you found that you could not cope with all the things that you had to do?
	- score-6:: 
- **7.** In the last month, how often have you been able to control irritations in your life? *(R)*
	- score-7:: 
- **8.** In the last month, how often have you felt that you were on top of things? *(R)*
	- score-8:: 
- **9.** In the last month, how often have you been angered because of things that were outside of your control?
	- score-9:: 
- **10.** In the last month, how often have you felt difficulties were piling up so high that you could not overcome them?
	- score-10:: 
- ---
- **Total score::** 
- ---
- ## Scoring
- Reverse-scored items: 4, 5, 7, 8. Items 4, 5, 7, and 8 are reverse-scored: replace 0→4, 1→3, 2→2, 3→1, 4→0. Add all 10 scores. Total range: 0–40. Higher scores indicate greater perceived stress.
- ## Interpretation
	- 0–13 — Low stress
	- 14–26 — Moderate stress
	- 27–40 — High perceived stress
