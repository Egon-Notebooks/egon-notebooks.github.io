- # RSES — Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale
- *Recommended frequency: monthly or quarterly.*
- ---
- **Please read each statement and indicate how strongly you agree or disagree with it right now.**
- *Scale: 3 = Strongly agree | 2 = Agree | 1 = Disagree | 0 = Strongly disagree*
- *Items marked (R) are reverse-scored — see the scoring note below.*
- ---

	- score-3:: 
- **4.** I am able to do things as well as most other people.
	- score-4::

	- score-5::

	- score-6::

	- score-7:: 
- **8.** I wish I could have more respect for myself. *(R)*
	- score-8:: 
- **9.** All in all, I am inclined to feel that I am a failure. *(R)*
	- score-9:: 
- **10.** I take a positive attitude toward myself.
	- score-10::

- ## Scoring
- Reverse-scored items: 2, 5, 6, 8, 9. Items 2, 5, 6, 8, and 9 are reverse-scored: replace 0→3, 1→2, 2→1, 3→0. Add all 10 scores. Total range: 0–30.
- ## Interpretation
	- 0–14 — Low self-esteem
	- 15–25 — Within the normal range
	- 26–30 — High self-esteem
