- ## Scoring
- Add the scores for all 7 items. Total range: 0–21.
- ## Interpretation
	- 0–4 — Minimal anxiety
	- 5–9 — Mild anxiety
	- 10–14 — Moderate anxiety
	- 15–21 — Severe anxiety
