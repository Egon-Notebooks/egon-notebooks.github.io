- # BRS — Brief Resilience Scale
- *Recommended frequency: monthly or quarterly.*
- ---
- **Please indicate the extent to which you agree with each of the following statements.**
- *Scale: 1 = Strongly disagree | 2 = Disagree | 3 = Neutral | 4 = Agree | 5 = Strongly agree*
- *Items marked (R) are reverse-scored — see the scoring note below.*
- ---
- **1.** I tend to bounce back quickly after hard times.
	- score-1:: 
- **2.** I have a hard time making it through stressful events. *(R)*
	- score-2:: 
- **3.** It does not take me long to recover from a stressful event.
	- score-3:: 
- **4.** It is hard for me to snap back when something bad happens. *(R)*
	- score-4:: 
- **5.** I usually come through difficult times with little trouble.
	- score-5:: 
- **6.** I tend to take a long time to get over set-backs in my life. *(R)*
	- score-6:: 
- ---
- **Total score::** 
- ---
- ## Scoring
- Reverse-scored items: 2, 4, 6. Items 2, 4, and 6 are reverse-scored: replace 1→5, 2→4, 3→3, 4→2, 5→1. Add all 6 scores and divide by 6 to get your average. Range: 1.00–5.00.
- ## Interpretation
	- 1.00–2.99 — Low resilience
	- 3.00–4.30 — Normal resilience
	- 4.31–5.00 — High resilience
