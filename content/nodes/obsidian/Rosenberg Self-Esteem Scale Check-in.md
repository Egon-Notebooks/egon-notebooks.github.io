# RSES — Rosenberg Self-Esteem Scale

*Recommended frequency: monthly or quarterly.*

---

## Scoring

Reverse-scored items: 2, 5, 6, 8, 9. Items 2, 5, 6, 8, and 9 are reverse-scored: replace 0→3, 1→2, 2→1, 3→0. Add all 10 scores. Total range: 0–30.

## Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation |
| --- | --- |
| 0–14 | Low self-esteem |
| 15–25 | Within the normal range |
| 26–30 | High self-esteem |
